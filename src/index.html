Who I am

I started programming as a young teen, starting from Scratch — and that sentiment persisted throughout my journey. I've always preferred to understand everything to the core and build hands-on. But this reluctance towards libraries and frameworks steadily transformed into a joy of learning, and exploring concepts that I would've never had the chance to (re)invent myself.

I got into music production in 2020. By 2022, at the age of 17, I had already landed my first job as an audiobook editor in a company on the other side of the globe. I won the audition, and Steve sent through a contract. This meant fulfilling my dreams of contributing to the audio industry and discovering my impact — the books I'd worked on amassed a combined six-figure listen count. For the first time in my life, I was leaving my indelible fingerprint on the world — our world.
